###Controls###

Common:
-Start or P to hide UI controls (in android if using an external controller or keyboard)

3D game: 
-WASD/LeftStick to move
-Arrows/RightStick to move the camera
-Spacebar/B-button to jump
-E key/RightTrigger/Touch the icon on the bottom right(on android) to consume a powerup

2D games:
- Shown on screen
